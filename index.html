About Zen Protocol

Decentralization & Utility above all

$ZEN token was designed with heavy utility in mind so that our investors can sleep in peace knowing that regardless of makret conditions, $ZEN will be doing well as DeFi exists. Zen Protocol charges a small fee between %0.1-%1 on all our services and buys back $ZEN token with it along with a 0.1 burn.

  • Pioneering self-repaying loans with FaaS. Deposit your stablecoins with us and allow our seasoned DeFi trader to pay back your loan, this allows for better flexiability .
  • Automated Portofolio manager AKA index funds: Exchange tokens more efficiently and Deposit liquidity for up to 8 tokens, and allow arbitrage bots and users to rebalance your portofolio, generating a small fee for liquidity providers.
  • Single-sided liquidity pools: provide liquidity while protecting against impermanent loss
  • No loss lottery: Deposit your tokens and qualify for a chance at winning a big prize every two weeks without the risk of losing your hard earned tokens.
  • Bootstrapping liquidity pools: Allows token developers to launch with much less capital than traditional methods, making Zen Protocol the true decentralized launchpad of Aurora.
